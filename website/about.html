HALTON WALDROF SCHOOL

WELCOME

"Founded in 1984, Halton Waldrof School is accredited Waldrof co-educational school serving students in Bangalore urban and surrounding area.
We offer programs from parent and child through grade 10 and will have a full high school by the 2022-2023 school year.
HWS is a place to grow intellectually, artistically, and socially.(grade school current; high school in process)".

"VISION"
- HWS believes in imparting knowledge/values and nurturing talent, enthusiasm and creativity of its students for seeking excellence through high qualityeducational endeavours.

"MISSION"
- To pursue excellence and set the pace in the field of school education;
- To develop the spirit of national integration and create a sense of "Indianness" among children.

"MANAGEMENT"
The HALTON WALDROF SCHOOL is registered as a society under the Societies Registration Act (XXI of 1860).
The primary aim of the instituition is to provide,establish,endow,maintain control and manage the Schools located all over India.
The Instituition has three tier management structure with its (I) Headquarters (II) Regional Offices and (III) HWS spread all over the country each having their Manangement Committees.

HALTON WALDROF SCHOOL has classes from I to XII with an enrollment of 2200.
